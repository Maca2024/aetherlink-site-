Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Who we are

AetherLink.ai is an AI consultancy and technology company based in the Netherlands. We provide AI solutions, consultancy and development for businesses across Europe. AetherLink is the data controller responsible for the processing of personal data as described in this privacy policy.

Chamber of Commerce (KVK) number: [to be added]

2. What data we collect

We may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact details: name and email address when you fill in our contact form or email us.
  • Chat data: messages you send through AetherAssist, our AI chat assistant on the website.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information and page visits via analytics.

3. Purpose of data processing

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: to answer your questions and deliver our services.
  • Communication: to contact you in response to your request.
  • Improvement: to analyse and improve our website and services.

4. Retention period

We do not retain your personal data longer than strictly necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. Contact details are deleted once the communication is concluded, unless there is an ongoing business relationship. Technical data is anonymised after a maximum of 26 months.

5. Sharing with third parties

We never sell your data to third parties. We only use the following processors to deliver our services:

  • Vercel: hosting of our website (US, EU Standard Contractual Clauses).
  • Anthropic: AI processing for AetherAssist chat functionality (US, EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

6. Cookies

Our website only uses technically necessary cookies that are essential for the functioning of the website (such as session cookies and theme preference). We do not place tracking cookies, marketing cookies or third-party cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: you can request to see what data we process about you.
  • Right to rectification: you can request that incorrect data be corrected.
  • Right to erasure: you can request that your data be deleted.
  • Right to object: you can object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: you can request to receive your data in a structured format.

You can exercise your rights by contacting us at info@aetherlink.ai. We will respond to your request within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

8. Security

We take the protection of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to prevent misuse, loss, unauthorised access and unwanted disclosure. Our website uses HTTPS encryption, all data is stored encrypted and access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel.
