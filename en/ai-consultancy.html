The Discipline

What is
AI Consultancy?

AI consultancy is more than recommending tools or building chatbots. It is the strategic discipline of aligning artificial intelligence with your organisation's goals, culture, and capabilities. True AI consultancy addresses the fundamental questions: where can AI create the most value, how do we implement it responsibly, and how do we ensure our people embrace the change?

At AetherLink, we believe AI consultancy must go beyond technology. The organisations that succeed with AI are those that combine technical excellence with genuine human understanding. That is why our approach integrates strategy, implementation, and change management into a single coherent journey.

Assessment

AI Readiness Scan

Before you can move forward with AI, you need to know where you stand. Our AI Readiness Scan provides a comprehensive assessment of your organisation's current position across four critical dimensions — resulting in a concrete report with priorities and a phased roadmap.

1

Processes & Workflows

We map your current processes and identify where AI can deliver the greatest impact. Which workflows are repetitive? Where are the bottlenecks? What decisions could be augmented?

2

Data & Infrastructure

AI runs on data. We assess the quality, accessibility, and structure of your data landscape. We evaluate your technical infrastructure and identify gaps that need addressing.

3

Team & Skills

We evaluate your team's current AI literacy, willingness to adopt new technologies, and identify skill gaps. This informs both the training programme and the change management approach.

4

Strategy & Governance

We review your existing AI ambitions, governance structures, and compliance readiness. The result is a clear picture of strategic maturity and a prioritised roadmap for next steps.
